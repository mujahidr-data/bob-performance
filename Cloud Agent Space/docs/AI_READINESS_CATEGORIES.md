## ğŸ¯ Assessment Framework

### Rating Scale

**AI-Ready (Meets Criteria)**
- Demonstrates 3+ of the 5 AI readiness criteria
- Shows measurable productivity/quality improvements with AI
- Proactively adopts and evangelizes AI tools
- Contributes to AI-first culture

**AI-Capable (Developing)**
- Demonstrates 2 of the 5 AI readiness criteria
- Uses AI tools but limited impact
- Open to AI adoption but needs coaching
- Potential to reach AI-Ready with support

**Not AI-Ready (Traditional)**
- Demonstrates 0-1 of the 5 AI readiness criteria
- Resistant to AI tool adoption
- Manual-first approach to work
- Requires significant reskilling or role adjustment
